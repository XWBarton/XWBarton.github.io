Booking in Cryptick Lab

  1. Go to https://calendar.google.com
  1. Make sure the calendars are selected (Fig. 1)
  1. Click "+" or "Create"
  1. Click "Event"
  1. Name the Event the hood / equipment you wish to book with your name (list below)
  1. Select your date and time
  1. Change to calendar to select the correct room (Fig. 2 outlined in red)
  1. Click "Save"
